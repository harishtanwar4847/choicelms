Spark Advantages
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Simple overdraft loan

Loan against shares at SPARK is in form of an overdraft credit line which you can use at your ease. Withdraw and Repay loan amounts as per your requirement. Interest is charged only on the amount withdrawn and not on the entire overdraft limit.

Smart & Easy Loans

Smart loan top-up:
You get easy one click loan top up benefit whenever there is increase in value of pledged securities.

Pledge/Unpledge:
Get rid of tedious pledge/unpledge formalities by experiencing simple and seamless process at Spark.

Sell securities:
Now you can Sell your pledged securities through SPARK to get instant credit to your loan account.

Easy tracking of portfolio:
You can now track your portfolio of pledged securities and get instant notification of any decrease in value to take immediate remedial action.

Complete control over your loans

You can have full control over your loans and underlying collateral securities and keep track of the same at your fingertips.

Lower interest rates than margin funding

Traders can now increase profits on trades by getting rid of high interest rates charged on margin funding. Avail easy loan against your securities at much lower interest rates.

No hidden Fees

Simple & Quick Process

Easy Documentation
