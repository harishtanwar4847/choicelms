About Us

SPARK.loans is a digital lending platform that offers loans against securities.

Any asset which is in demat / electronic form can be used as a security for obtaining loan through the SPARK platform. The loan is in form of an overdraft limit (running account facility) which the borrower can utilize as and when required with no burden of monthly EMI payments.

We aim to leverage the power of technology and data science to keep on innovating, customizing and improving our product for the benefit of the customers.
